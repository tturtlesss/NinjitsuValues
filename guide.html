Trading Guide

Value


Value is the amount someone is willing to pay for a fruit/gamepass in a trade.

When determining the value of a fruit, it is important to note that each fruit has two separate values: Regular Fruit Value and Permanent Fruit Value. It is crucial to use the correct value when trading.

When evaluating the fairness of a trade, it is important to consider both the value and demand for the item in question. We encourage you to carefully review the values of the items you wish to trade.


Demand


Demand refers to player interest in an item. The higher the rating, the higher the desirability.

The demand for each item is rated on a scale of 1 to 10. If an item has a rating of 1 to 4, it means low demand. A rating of 5 to 7 means moderate demand. A rating of 8 to 10 means high demand.
