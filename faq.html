Why is there colored text on each value card?

The colored text on each value card represents the rarity of the item. Different colors correspond to different rarity tiers (e.g., Common, Rare, Epic, Legendary, Mythical).

What impact does demand have on value and trading?

Demand plays a crucial role in determining an item's value in trading. High demand for a particular item can drive its value up, while low demand can lower it.

Why do some scrolls/items have similar values but different demands?

Even if scrolls/items have similar base values, their demand can differ due to various factors such as in-game utility, aesthetic appeal, or popularity among players.

I can't find a certain scroll/item on the website. What do I do?

If you can't find a certain scroll/item on the website, it might be a newly released item that hasn't been added yet, or it could be a less common item. You can try checking back later or contacting the website administrators to request its addition.

How do I report a bug?

To report a bug, you can usually contact the website administrators through discord @tturtlesss. Be sure to provide as much detail as possible about the bug, including steps to reproduce it and any relevant screenshots.
